Stay with the tool that knows you

We built Absent to be the absence sidekick you keep coming back to. A living timeline tracks every submitted form, reminders nudge you before deadlines, and personal notes make sure nothing is forgotten.

  • Keep a searchable archive of every absence request.

Why another app?

Because variety matters. Our friends at absendo.app already offer a solid solution. Absent is our playful experiment to explore different ideas, smoother onboarding, and retention-focused features.

Competition keeps us all sharp, and you benefit from better tools no matter which one you choose.

Friendly heads-up

Absent is a fun side project created by students for students. There is no commercial backing, just curiosity and late-night coding sessions. Expect transparency, rapid iteration, and the occasional rough edge.
