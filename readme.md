# Absent

**Absence forms generated in seconds. From students, for students.**

### ğŸ“– What is Absent?

**Absent** is a free web tool designed to make the life of vocational students (Lernende) in Switzerland easier. We all know the hassle: you miss a day of school due to illness or an appointment, and you have to manually fill out a PDF form, look up which lessons you missed, find the exact names of your teachers, and format everything correctly.

**Absent automates this entire process.**

By connecting to your **Schulnetz** calendar, Absent automatically detects which lessons you missed on a specific date and fills out the official school form (Entschuldigung / Urlaubsgesuch) for you. What used to take 10 minutes of typing and searching now takes less than 60 seconds.
